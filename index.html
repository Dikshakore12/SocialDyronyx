FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long before I see results?+
Most clients see measurable results within 30-60 days. Social media growth shows clear trends within 4-6 weeks. Google review numbers improve significantly in the first 2-3 weeks of automation.
Is there a contract or lock-in?+
No lock-in contracts. All plans are month-to-month. We believe in earning your business every single month through results, not paperwork.
Can I switch plans later?+
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. If you upgrade, the difference is prorated for the rest of your billing period.
How does Google Review automation work?+
Our platform sends automated WhatsApp messages and emails to your customers after their service experience, with a direct link to leave a Google review. It also filters negative feedback before it goes public, keeping your rating high.
Will fake reviews get my account banned?+
No. We only request reviews from your real customers — we never use bots, fake accounts, or incentivized reviews. Our system is fully compliant with Google's review policies.
Do you work with businesses outside Aurangabad?+
Yes! We work with businesses across India. Most of our work is done remotely, and we have clients from Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Nashik, Hyderabad, and beyond.
